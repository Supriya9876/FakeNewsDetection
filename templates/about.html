About Us

We are a group of six passionate final-year B.Tech students specializing in Computer Science and Engineering. As part of our final year project, we have embarked on a mission to address the growing problem of misinformation through our project, "Fake News Detection using Machine Learning." Our team is dedicated to leveraging our technical skills and knowledge in machine learning to develop a reliable system that can accurately differentiate between fake and real news. With the rise of digital media, the spread of false information has become a critical issue, and we believe our project can contribute to mitigating this problem. Throughout our academic journey, we have gained extensive experience in various aspects of computer science, including data analysis, algorithm development, and software engineering. This project is the culmination of our studies, combining our expertise and collaborative efforts to create a meaningful and impactful tool.

Thank you for visiting, and we hope you find our tools and resources helpful in the fight against fake news.
